Dengue is one of the most crucial arthropod-borne viral diseases that affects humans in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.
Malaysia is one of the South-East Asia country that is affected by dengue infection.
The number of dengue fever (DF) and dengue haemorrhagic fever (DHF) cases in Malaysia shows an increasing trend, especiallly in urban and semi urban areas.
It happens in areas where there is rapid development activities and high population density.
